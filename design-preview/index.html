What changed (App Store fixes)

Camera permission auto-requests on mount; "Go Back" pre-prompt removed (5.1.1(iv)). Pokemon refs scrubbed across all source: pokemon-tcg.ts deleted, demo cards (Charizard/Blastoise/Pikachu/Mewtwo) and images.pokemontcg.io URLs removed. "PSA" → "Grade" in user-visible labels; "Trainer Tips" → "Scanning Tips"; "Pokéball palette" → "Brand palette". Disclaimer added on every result.

Design system

Single saturated accent: mint #34D9A0. Gold #FFC93C reserved for Gem Mint reveals only. True-black canvas #050507. Inter font with stylistic-set 01 + cv11 alternates. Liquid-Glass surfaces (44 px blur, 180% saturation) for tab bar + sticky CTAs.
